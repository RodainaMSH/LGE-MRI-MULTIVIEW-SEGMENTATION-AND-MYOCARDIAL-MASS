TECHNICAL INNOVATIONS THAT WORKED
================================================================================

1. Focal Loss (gamma=2.0):
   - Down-weights easy examples (background)
   - Focuses on hard examples (scar boundaries)
   - Result: Better boundary precision

2. Class Weighting (Scar: 10x):
   - Forces model to pay attention to tiny scar regions
   - Prevents scar from being ignored
   - Result: Improved scar recall

3. LR Warm-up (5 epochs):
   - Prevents early instability
   - Smoother loss curves
   - Result: Better final convergence

4. Extended Training (50 epochs):
   - More time to learn scar patterns
   - Learning rate reductions at epochs 20, 30, 40
   - Result: +0.06 Dice improvement
